# Imaginal Exposure Session Scaffold 子技能地图

## 子技能列表

- [Exposure Hot Spot Identification Protocol](心理咨询/Family技能/行为主义/微技能/Exposure Hot Spot Identification Protocol/SKILL.md) ｜ 微技能
  - 适用：A therapist micro-skill to identify, label, and flag high-arousal moments ('hot spots') within a trauma narrative during imaginal exposure for later targeted processing.
  - 线索：Client pauses unusually long (>5 sec) during memory narration, Client displays acute somatic response (sweating, tearfulness, voice breaking) without prompting, SUDS spikes ≥30 points within one narrative clause, imaginal exposure, PTSD
- [SUDS-Guided Exposure Pacing](心理咨询/Family技能/行为主义/微技能/SUDS-Guided Exposure Pacing/SKILL.md) ｜ 微技能
  - 适用：A micro-intervention where the therapist uses real-time SUDS ratings to calibrate pacing, validate distress, and decide whether to continue, pause, or repeat segments of imaginal exposure.
  - 线索：Client provides a SUDS rating ≥70 mid-exposure, Client pauses or shows physiological arousal (sweating, tearfulness), Therapist observes incongruence between reported SUDS and behavioral cues, imaginal exposure, SUDS
