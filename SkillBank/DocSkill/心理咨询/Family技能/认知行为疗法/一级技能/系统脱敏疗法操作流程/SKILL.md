# 系统脱敏疗法操作流程

一个结构化的暴露-放松渐进式焦虑消退流程，用于帮助求助者逐步脱敏特定可分级的焦虑刺激源。

## Prompt

1. Collaboratively construct an anxiety hierarchy (5–10 items) anchored to the client's specific trigger (e.g., initiating a text to a romantic interest, going on a first date). 2. Teach and rehearse progressive muscle relaxation or diaphragmatic breathing until client achieves reliable self-induction (≥3/5 mastery rating). 3. Begin imaginal exposure at the lowest hierarchy item while maintaining relaxed state; hold until SUDS drops ≥50% or reaches ≤2. 4. Repeat until habituation occurs (two consecutive trials with SUDS ≤1), then advance to next item. 5. Transition to in vivo exposure only after full imaginal habituation across all items. 6. Assign daily relaxation practice + hierarchy item rehearsal as between-session task.

## Objective

reduce stimulus-bound anxiety through systematic desensitization
## Applicable Signals

- client identifies discrete, repeatable anxiety-eliciting situations
- client demonstrates willingness to practice relaxation daily
- client reports anxiety is situationally bound rather than free-floating

## Contraindications

- client has active panic disorder with unpredictable surges
- client lacks capacity to practice relaxation outside session

## Intervention Moves

- hierarchy co-construction
- relaxation induction training
- imaginal exposure sequencing
- in vivo exposure progression
- SUDS monitoring

## Workflow Steps

- Construct individualized anxiety hierarchy
- Teach and assess mastery of relaxation technique
- Conduct imaginal exposure starting from lowest hierarchy item
- Confirm habituation before advancing to next item
- Transition to in vivo exposure after full imaginal habituation
- Assign structured between-session practice

## Constraints

- Must not skip hierarchy levels
- Relaxation must be maintained throughout exposure trial
- No advancement until SUDS ≤1 sustained across two trials

## Cautions

- Avoid introducing new relaxation methods mid-hierarchy
- Do not proceed to in vivo without full imaginal habituation
- Monitor for avoidance disguised as 'relaxation' (e.g., dissociation)

## Output Contract

- Client reports zero subjective units of distress (SUDS ≤ 1) across all hierarchy items during imaginal or in vivo exposure

## Example Therapist Responses

### Example 1

- Client/Input: Client identifies 'asking someone out' as SUDS=8/10 and learns diaphragmatic breathing
- Therapist/Output: Client achieves SUDS≤1 on 'imagining sending the message' after three relaxed exposures
- Notes: Habituation confirmed before moving to 'rehearsing the words aloud'

### Example 2

- Client/Input: Client completes full imaginal hierarchy with SUDS≤1 on all items
- Therapist/Output: Client successfully attends a low-stakes group social event (in vivo level 3) with SUDS=1
- Notes: First in vivo trial conducted in-session with therapist support

## Objective

消除特定刺激引发的条件性焦虑反应
## Applicable Signals

- 求助者能清晰描述焦虑触发场景并排序强度
- 求助者在指导语下可完成5分钟渐进式肌肉放松且报告身体松弛感
- SUDS量表使用稳定（0–10分）

## Contraindications

- 求助者当前处于急性危机或自杀风险状态
- 焦虑源不可具象化或无法分级
- 求助者拒绝暴露或放松练习

## Intervention Moves

- hierarchy co-construction
- relaxation induction training
- imaginal exposure sequencing
- in vivo exposure progression
- SUDS monitoring

## Workflow Steps

- 协同构建个体化焦虑等级表（含具体情境、感官细节、SUDS预估）
- 教学并练习深度肌肉放松（含呼吸同步、注意力锚定、放松反馈确认）
- 首次暴露：从最低级刺激开始，引导想象/实景接触+放松维持，记录SUDS变化与持续时间
- 阈值判定：SUDS ≤2且稳定维持≥30秒 → 升级；否则重复本级或微调刺激表述
- 逐级推进至最高级，全程记录各等级达标耗时与退阶次数
- 结束阶段：引导求助者复述‘我能在放松中面对X’的自我陈述，强化习得性控制感
- 在完全完成想象暴露所有等级后，过渡至结构化in vivo暴露

## Constraints

- 必须在建立稳固咨访关系后启动
- 每次暴露需以放松为前提，禁止在紧张状态下强行暴露
- 单次咨询最多推进2个等级，避免疲劳性脱敏失败
- 必须不跳级，放松须全程维持，无 advancement until SUDS ≤1 sustained across two trials (imaginal or in vivo)

## Cautions

- 警惕伪脱敏（表面平静但生理指标异常升高）
- 避免将等级表标准化，须由求助者自主生成并命名每一级刺激
- 若连续两次无法通过同一等级，需重新评估刺激定义或放松质量
- 避免引入新放松方法 mid-hierarchy
- 不得进入in vivo未完成全部imaginal habituation
- 监测回避伪装（如解离）

## Output Contract

- 求助者在最高级别刺激下维持肌肉放松且无主观焦虑感（SUDS ≤ 1）
